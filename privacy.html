Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026  ·  App: ProsyncHK – Private Portfolio Tracker

Short version: ProsyncHK does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial data on external servers. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

ProsyncHK – Private Portfolio Tracker ("the App") is developed and maintained by ProsyncHK Tech Company. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at prosynchk@gmail.com.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your portfolio holdings, transaction history, or financial data
  • Your device identifiers or location
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is saved only on your device using Android's secure local storage (AsyncStorage). It never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it:

  • Portfolio holdings, quantities, and costs you enter
  • Investment goals you set
  • App preferences (language, font size, display currency)
  • API keys you optionally provide (Claude / Gemini) — stored locally and used solely to make requests from your device to the respective AI provider

4. Third-Party Services

The App optionally connects to the following third-party services. These connections are initiated directly from your device:

  • Yahoo Finance — to fetch live stock prices and dividend data. No personal data is sent; only stock ticker symbols are queried.
  • Google Gemini API — if you choose to add a Gemini API key, your portfolio summary and questions are sent to Google's API for AI analysis. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — if you choose to add a Claude API key, your portfolio summary and questions are sent to Anthropic's API. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — the free version of the App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers for ad personalisation. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

5. API Keys

If you choose to enter an API key (Claude or Gemini), it is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. We never transmit your API key to our servers. The key is used solely to make API calls directly from your device to the respective provider.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own protection (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

8. Data Deletion

To delete all App data, simply uninstall the App from your device. This will permanently remove all locally stored portfolio data, preferences, and API keys.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
