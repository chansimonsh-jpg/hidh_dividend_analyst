關於 HiDH Dividend Analyst

HiDH Dividend Analyst 是一個專注於全球高息股分析的獨立資訊平台，涵蓋香港、美國及英國三大市場，每個交易日自動更新數據，為投資者提供系統化的股息股篩選參考。

我們相信，長期穩定的股息收入是財富增長的重要基石。透過量化評分系統，我們對數百隻股票進行全面分析，幫助投資者發掘真正具備長線持有價值的優質高息股。

我們的分析方法

每隻股票均以100分制進行綜合評分，涵蓋股息質量、估值、財務健康、增長潛力及技術走勢五大範疇，務求客觀、全面地評估每隻股票的投資價值。

About Us

About HiDH Dividend Analyst

HiDH Dividend Analyst is an independent information platform focused on global high-dividend stock analysis, covering Hong Kong, US, and UK markets. Data is updated automatically every trading day to provide investors with a systematic dividend stock screening reference.

We believe that long-term stable dividend income is an important cornerstone of wealth growth. Through our quantitative scoring system, we comprehensively analyse hundreds of stocks to help investors identify quality high-dividend stocks truly worth holding for the long term.

Our Methodology

Each stock is scored on a 100-point scale across five dimensions: dividend quality, valuation, financial health, growth potential, and technical trends — providing an objective and comprehensive assessment of each stock's investment value.
