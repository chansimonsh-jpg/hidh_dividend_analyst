關於 HiDH Dividend Analyst

HiDH Dividend Analyst 是一個專注於全球高息股分析的獨立資訊平台，涵蓋香港、美國、英國及中國A股四大市場，每個交易日自動更新數據，為投資者提供系統化的股息股篩選參考。

我們相信，長期穩定的股息收入是財富增長的重要基石。透過量化評分系統，我們對數百隻股票進行全面分析，幫助投資者發掘真正具備長線持有價值的優質高息股。

我們的分析方法

每隻股票均以100分制進行綜合評分，涵蓋五大範疇：

  • 股息質量（30分）：息率水平、派息穩定性及現金流覆蓋率
  • 估值（25分）：現價相對歷史息率及市場的吸引程度
  • 財務健康（25分）：資產負債、現金流及償債能力
  • 增長（10分）：股息增長趨勢及盈利前景
  • 技術走勢（10分）：RSI、52週位置等技術指標

評級說明

根據總分，每隻股票會獲得以下評級：

  • 🟢🟢 強力買入（75分以上）：各方面均表現優秀，值得重點關注
  • 🟢 值得關注（60–74分）：基本面良好，可納入觀察名單
  • ⚖️ 觀望（50–59分）：有一定吸引力，但需留意風險

🔴 高危高危名單說明

高危名單列出評分最低的股票，供投資者參考迴避。高危股通常具備以下特徵：派息比率過高甚至超過盈利、現金流無法支撐股息、財務槓桿過高、近期股息曾被削減，或估值嚴重偏高。

高危名單並非「沽出」建議，而是提示投資者相關股票存在較高風險，需作更深入研究才考慮持有。

About Us

About HiDH Dividend Analyst

HiDH Dividend Analyst is an independent platform focused on global high-dividend stock analysis, covering Hong Kong, US, UK, and China A-share markets. Data is updated every trading day to provide investors with a systematic dividend stock screening reference.

We believe that long-term stable dividend income is an important cornerstone of wealth growth. Through our quantitative scoring system, we comprehensively analyse hundreds of stocks to help investors identify quality high-dividend stocks truly worth holding long-term.

Our Methodology

Each stock is scored on a 100-point scale across five dimensions:

  • Dividend Quality (30pts): Yield level, payout stability and free cash flow coverage
  • Valuation (25pts): Current price vs historical yield and attractiveness
  • Financial Health (25pts): Balance sheet, cash flow and debt coverage
  • Growth (10pts): Dividend growth trend and earnings outlook
  • Technical (10pts): RSI, 52-week position and other indicators

Rating Guide

Each stock receives a rating based on its total score:

  • 🟢🟢 Strong Buy (75+): Excellent across all dimensions, high priority
  • 🟢 Watch (60–74): Good fundamentals, worth monitoring
  • ⚖️ Hold (50–59): Some appeal, monitor risks carefully

🔴 High-RiskHigh-Risk List Explained

The High-Risk List highlights the lowest-scoring stocks for investors to approach with caution. High-risk stocks typically exhibit: payout ratios exceeding earnings, cash flow unable to sustain dividends, excessive financial leverage, recent dividend cuts, or severely stretched valuations.

This list is not a sell recommendation — it flags stocks requiring deeper research before any investment decision.
